Djikstra

Add a vertex by clicking on the canvas below. The position of the Vertex is the position of your mouse.

Add an edge between two vertices by filling out the three boxes below. Use the number of the vertex in the canvas to identify it. As an example, to add an edge between the vertex "1" and the vertex "2" with a weight of 5, write 1 in the first box, 2 in the second, and 5 in the third. Then press the "Add Edge" button.
