Djikstra

Add a vertex by left-clicking on the canvas below. The position of the Vertex is the position of your mouse. Remove a vertex by right-clicking on the given vertex.

Add an edge between two vertices by filling out the three boxes below. Use the number of the vertex in the canvas to identify it. As an example, to add an edge between the vertex "0" and the vertex "1" with a weight of 5, write 1 in the first box, 2 in the second, and 5 in the third. Then press the "Add Edge" button. The "Remove Edge" button uses the same principle. Only no edge weight needs to be given.

The button "Reset Graph" clears the canvas.
